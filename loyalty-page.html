How BlkPoints Work

BlkPoints are your BlkPages reward credits — you earn them automatically every time you book, refer a friend, or leave a verified review.

🪙 How You Earn BlkPoints
  • You'll earn 1 BlkPoint for every £1 you spend on completed bookings
  • Points appear as pending for 24 hours after your appointment — this allows time for any cancellations or adjustments
  • After 24 hours, your points become confirmed and available to spend
  • You can also earn 25 BlkPoints for a verified review and 100 BlkPoints when a friend you refer completes their first booking
  • Points don't apply to cancelled or refunded bookings
💰 Using BlkPoints
  • You can redeem BlkPoints in £5 increments (500 points = £5)
  • Your BlkPoints discount applies straight away at checkout
  • The points stay pending for 24 hours after your service is completed — this protects you in case a booking is cancelled or changed
  • Once that 24-hour period passes, your points are confirmed and permanently deducted
  • If your booking is cancelled or refunded before that time, your BlkPoints will automatically return to your balance
⏳ Other Details
  • You'll always see the status of your redemptions — Pending, Confirmed, or Released — in your dashboard
  • Points expire after 12 months of inactivity
  • BlkPoints have no cash value and can only be used on BlkPages bookings
  • BlkPages may adjust or remove points for cancellations, refunds, or misuse
