How BlkPoints Work

BlkPoints are your BlkPages reward credits — you earn them automatically every time you book, refer a friend, or leave a verified review.

🪙 Earning BlkPoints
  • +1 point for every £1 spent on a completed booking
  • +100 points when someone you refer completes their first booking
  • +25 points for each verified review (credited 24 hours after verification)
  • Points are added 24 hours after your appointment ends, once it's marked completed
  • Cancelled or refunded bookings won't earn points
💰 Using BlkPoints
  • 100 points = £1 credit toward your next booking
  • Redeem from 500 points (£5), in £5 increments
  • You can use points to cover up to 50% of a booking
  • The booking must be worth at least 2× your redemption value
    (e.g. to redeem £10 in BlkPoints, your booking must total £20 or more)
  • Points are applied automatically at checkout when you choose to use them
⏳ Other Details
  • Points expire after 12 months of inactivity
  • BlkPoints have no cash value and can only be used on BlkPages bookings
  • BlkPages may adjust or remove points for cancellations, refunds, or misuse
