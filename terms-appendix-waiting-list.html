Availability

The Waiting List Offer ("WLO") feature is available only to businesses on the Â£29/month Premium Plan. Free or basic accounts do not include this feature. Upgrading to the Premium Plan activates WLO automatically on eligible services.

Purpose

WLO helps fill cancelled or rescheduled appointments by offering newly available slots to customers who joined the waiting list for a specific service.

How It Works

  • Each business can decide which services include the waiting list option.
  • When a service is fully booked, customers can join the waiting list from the business's profile page.
  • When a slot becomes available, the system automatically offers it to the next eligible customer.
  • Each offer is held for two (2) hours; after that, it passes automatically to the next customer in the queue.
  • All offers are sent automatically by BlkPages via email and SMS; businesses do not send these messages manually.

Customer Consent

  • Only customers who have opted in for waiting list notifications will receive offers.
  • Each message explains why the customer is being contacted and includes a link to update preferences or opt out.
  • Customers may opt out at any time through their account settings.

Quiet Hours

  • Notifications are sent between 08:00 and 21:00 (local time) to avoid disturbance.
  • Messages queued overnight send automatically at 08:00.

Business Responsibilities

  • Keep service availability and cancellation data accurate in your dashboard.
  • WLO relies on correct schedules; BlkPages is not responsible for mismanaged or missed offers.

Purpose

Customers can join a waiting list for any fully booked service and receive a one-time notification if a matching slot becomes available.

  • The offer is valid for two (2) hours. If not booked within that time, it automatically passes to the next person.
  • Notifications are sent by BlkPages; businesses do not contact customers directly.

3. Business Cancellation & Amendment Policy

Purpose

This policy defines when businesses can cancel or amend bookings while protecting customers from unfair disruption.

Permitted Circumstances

Businesses may cancel or change bookings only for genuine operational reasons, such as:

  • Staff illness or emergency closure.
  • Equipment or facility failure (power cuts, floods, etc.).
  • Duplicate or fraudulent bookings.
  • Customer misconduct (e.g., repeated no-shows).

Timing Controls

  • Businesses can cancel or amend only if the booking start time is more than 24 hours away.
  • Cancellations within 24 hours require review by BlkPages Support.

Refund Rules

  • For prepaid bookings, BlkPages automatically issues a full or partial refund through the payment processor.
  • Businesses cannot retain or reuse the funds.
  • For "Pay at Venue" bookings, cancellations are logged automatically.
  • If cancellations exceed 5% of total bookings in 30 days, the account may be flagged for review.

Customer Notifications

  • BlkPages sends automatic messages to customers for every cancellation or amendment, confirming the reason and refund status.
  • Businesses cannot message customers directly outside the system.

Amendments

  • Businesses cannot directly change a booking's date, time, or price.
  • They may send a change request through BlkPages; the customer must accept it.
  • All actions and responses are logged for transparency.

Audit and Review

  • Each cancellation or change creates an audit entry (who, when, why).
  • BlkPages monitors cancellation rates and reserves the right to issue warnings, pause features, or suspend accounts if misuse occurs.

Dashboard Reminder (Short Notice)

Cancellations & Changes

  • You may cancel or amend bookings only for legitimate operational reasons.
  • All cancellations are logged and reviewed.
