About BLKPAGES

Our Mission

BLKPAGES is dedicated to connecting the community with exceptional Black and urban businesses in the beauty and grooming industry. We believe in the power of representation and the importance of supporting businesses that understand and celebrate our unique hair textures, styles, and beauty needs.

What We Do

We specialize in showcasing three core categories of beauty and grooming services:

  • Hairdressers: Professional hair styling, cutting, coloring, and treatment services
  • Barbers: Traditional and modern barbering services for all hair types
  • Beauty Services: Makeup, skincare, nail services, and beauty treatments

Why BLKPAGES?

Finding the right beauty and grooming professionals can be challenging, especially when looking for stylists who understand and work well with diverse hair textures and beauty needs. BLKPAGES eliminates the guesswork by connecting you with verified professionals who specialize in serving our community.

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Supporting Black and urban-owned businesses
  • Promoting diversity and inclusion in the beauty industry
  • Providing a platform for authentic community connections
  • Ensuring quality and professionalism in all listed businesses

Join Our Community

Whether you're looking for your next go-to stylist or you're a beauty professional ready to grow your clientele, BLKPAGES is here to connect our community. Together, we're building a stronger, more connected beauty ecosystem.
