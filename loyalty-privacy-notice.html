How we protect your data and respect your privacy

Data Controller

BlkPages Ltd is the data controller for your loyalty program data.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is handled securely in accordance with UK GDPR.

Legal Basis for Processing

Contract Performance (UK GDPR Art 6(1)(b))

We process your loyalty program data because it is necessary for the performance of our contract with you. This includes:

  • Tracking your loyalty progress
  • Generating reward vouchers
  • Managing your rewards
  • Communicating about your loyalty status

Data We Collect

Loyalty Program Data

  • Booking History: Services booked, dates, amounts
  • Loyalty Progress: Visits completed, spend amounts, progress tracking
  • Reward Vouchers: Generated codes, expiry dates, redemption status
  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone (for notifications)

Technical Data

  • Voucher Codes: Server-generated, non-guessable codes
  • Activity Logs: Timestamps of loyalty activities
  • Security Data: IP addresses, user agents (for security)

Data Security

Security Measures

  • Server-Side Generation: Voucher codes generated server-side only
  • Non-Guessable Codes: Cryptographically secure random generation
  • Unique Indexing: Database constraints prevent duplicate codes
  • Access Controls: Customers and businesses see only relevant records
  • No PII Exposure: Personal information not shared between users

Data Sharing

Limited Sharing

No PII Exposure: We do not share personal information between customers and businesses.

Business View: Businesses see only voucher codes and redemption status.

Customer View: Customers see only their own loyalty data.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your loyalty data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Delete your loyalty data
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Data Portability: Export your loyalty data
  • Object: Opt-out of loyalty tracking

Opt-Out of Loyalty Tracking

You Can Opt-Out Anytime

If you choose to opt-out of loyalty tracking:

  • Your loyalty progress will be reset
  • Any unredeemed vouchers will be cancelled
  • We will stop tracking your loyalty activities
  • You can opt back in at any time
Opt-Out of Loyalty Tracking

Data Retention

How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Loyalty Progress: Until you opt-out or complete the program
  • Reward Vouchers: Until expiry or redemption
  • Activity Logs: 7 years for security and compliance
  • Contact Information: Until you opt-out or delete your account
