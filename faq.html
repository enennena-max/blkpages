Finding Businesses

How do I find businesses near me?
Click the "Near Me" button to automatically find businesses in your area using your location, or enter your city, area, or postcode in the search box.
How do I search by category?
Use the category dropdown to filter by Hairdressers, Barbers, or Beauty Services. You can also click on the category cards on the homepage.
Can I filter by rating?
Yes! On the search results page, use the rating filter to show only businesses with 4+ stars, 5 stars, or any rating you prefer.
What are featured listings?
Featured listings are premium businesses that appear at the top of search results. They have enhanced profiles with photos, reviews, and booking options.

Business Listings

What's the difference between Free and Premium listings?
Free listings show basic information (name, address, phone). Premium listings include customer reviews, photos, online booking, priority placement, and analytics.
How do I list my business?
Click "List Your Business" to create your free listing. Fill out your business information, choose your plan, and you're ready to go!
How much does it cost to list my business?
Basic listings are completely free! Premium listings cost £19/month and include advanced features like reviews, photos, and online booking.
Can I upgrade my listing later?
Absolutely! You can upgrade from Free to Premium at any time through your business dashboard. You can also downgrade if needed.

Reviews & Ratings

How do I leave a review?
Visit a business profile and scroll down to the reviews section. Click "Write a Review" to rate and share your experience.
Can I edit or delete my review?
Yes, you can edit or delete your review at any time. Go to the business profile and find your review to make changes.
How are ratings calculated?
Ratings are calculated as an average of all customer reviews. We use a 5-star system where 5 is excellent and 1 is poor.
Can businesses respond to reviews?
Yes! Premium businesses can respond to customer reviews to thank them or address any concerns.

Booking & Appointments

How do I book an appointment?
Look for the "Book Now" button on business profiles. Click it to see available times, select your service, and complete your booking.
Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?
Yes, you can manage your appointments through the booking system. Contact the business directly for any changes.
Do I need to pay when booking?
Some businesses require payment at booking, others allow you to pay at the appointment. This varies by business.
What if I can't find a business with online booking?
Not all businesses offer online booking yet. You can call them directly using the phone number on their profile.

Loyalty Rewards Add-on

How is the £5/month add-on billed?
The Loyalty Rewards add-on is billed monthly alongside your subscription. If enabled, you are charged £5 immediately, and the add-on renews with your subscription.
What happens if I disable the add-on?
The add-on will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle. You won't be billed again unless you re-enable it.
Do you offer pro-rata refunds?
No. The £5/month fee is a flat charge. If you disable the add-on mid-month, it will remain active until the cycle ends, but no partial refunds are issued.
Can I re-enable the add-on later?
Yes, you can turn it back on at any time. You will be charged £5 immediately, and the renewal will align with your subscription cycle.
How do I earn loyalty rewards?
Each business sets their own loyalty programme. You might unlock a reward by booking a certain number of times, spending a certain amount, or booking within a timeframe.
How do I know if I'm close to unlocking a reward?
You can track your progress in your customer dashboard. You'll also receive an email (and SMS if enabled) when you're close.
How do I use my reward?
Rewards apply automatically at checkout once unlocked. You'll see the discount or free service before you confirm payment.
Do rewards expire?
Each business sets their own rules. Some rewards may not expire, while others may need to be used within a timeframe (e.g., 6 weeks or 6 months). Check the details in your dashboard.
Can I transfer rewards between businesses?
No. Rewards are tied to the business where you earned them.
Can I save a reward for later?
Yes. If you don't use it immediately, you can save it for your next booking (until expiry if the business has set one).
Do I need a code to redeem rewards?
No. Rewards are applied automatically at checkout when you book online.
