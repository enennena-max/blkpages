Waiting List Notifications

Terms and conditions for our waiting list system

Purpose

The waiting list system lets you reserve a place for a specific service that is currently fully booked. If a slot opens up, BlkPages will automatically notify you so you can book it before it's offered to someone else.

How It Works

  • You can join a waiting list for any service that shows the "Join Waiting List" option on a business's profile.
  • When a slot becomes available, the next person on the list will receive an email and SMS offer from BlkPages.
  • Each offer is held for two (2) hours. If you do not book within that time, the slot is automatically released to the next customer.
  • All messages are sent automatically by BlkPages on behalf of the business; the business does not contact you directly.

Data and Privacy

  • Your contact details (email and phone number) are encrypted and used only for booking-related notifications.
  • Businesses never see your full phone number or email address — only masked or anonymised versions.
  • Your details will not be shared with any third parties or used for marketing unless you have given separate consent for marketing messages.
  • Messages are sent during standard hours (08:00–21:00 local time) to avoid disturbance.

Consent

  • By joining a waiting list, you agree that BlkPages may contact you once when a matching slot becomes available.
  • You can opt out of waiting list alerts at any time by clicking "Manage Preferences" in the email or changing your settings in your customer dashboard.
  • Opting out will not affect your ability to make normal bookings.

Your Responsibilities

  • Please make sure your contact number and email are correct and verified in your account.
  • If your details are out of date, you may miss an offer or confirmation message.
  • Joining a waiting list does not guarantee that a slot will become available.

Fair Use

  • Waiting list notifications are limited to one offer per available slot to ensure fairness to all customers.
  • BlkPages may suspend or adjust the waiting list feature for maintenance or misuse prevention.

Important Notice

These terms are part of our overall Customer Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy. By using our waiting list system, you agree to these specific terms in addition to our general terms.

If you have any questions about waiting list notifications, please contact our support team.
