BlkPoints Terms & Conditions

Last updated: December 9, 2024

Back to Loyalty & Rewards

1. Overview

BlkPoints are BlkPages' reward currency that allows you to earn credits for various activities on our platform. These points can be redeemed for discounts on future bookings made through BlkPages.

Important Notice

BlkPoints have no cash value and cannot be exchanged for money. They can only be used to reduce the cost of bookings made through the BlkPages platform.

2. Earning Points

You can earn BlkPoints through various activities on the BlkPages platform. Points are automatically credited to your account based on the activities outlined below.

3. Redeeming Points

BlkPoints can be redeemed for discounts on eligible bookings made through the BlkPages platform. The redemption process and limitations are detailed below.

4. Earning BlkPoints

4.1 Eligibility

BlkPoints are earned only on completed, eligible bookings made through the BlkPages platform. Bookings made or paid for directly with a business outside of BlkPages do not qualify.

4.2 Earning Rate

  • You earn 1 BlkPoint for every £1 spent on a completed booking.
  • Points are calculated on the total amount paid (excluding any discounts, vouchers, or refunded amounts).

4.3 Referral Rewards

  • You will earn 100 BlkPoints (£1) when someone you refer completes their first booking through your unique referral link.
  • Referrals are counted once per new user.
  • You cannot refer yourself or an existing BlkPages user.

4.4 Review Rewards

  • You will earn 25 BlkPoints (£0.25) for each verified review you submit following a completed booking.
  • A review is considered "verified" once approved by BlkPages' moderation team.
  • Points for verified reviews are credited 24 hours after verification.

4.5 Booking Completion Rule

Points are credited automatically 24 hours after the scheduled end time of your appointment, provided that:

  • the booking has been completed and not cancelled, refunded, or disputed; and
  • the business has not raised an issue during the 24-hour post-service window.

4.6 Refunds and Cancellations

If a booking is cancelled, refunded, or disputed after completion, any BlkPoints earned from that booking will be automatically deducted from your account.

4.7 Discretionary Bonuses

BlkPages may issue promotional or bonus BlkPoints from time to time as part of marketing campaigns. Any additional conditions for these promotions will be communicated separately.

5. Redeeming BlkPoints

5.1 Conversion Rate

100 BlkPoints = £1.00 BlkCredit toward a future booking made through BlkPages.

5.2 Minimum Redemption Threshold

You must have at least 500 BlkPoints (£5.00) in your account to redeem.

5.3 Redemption Increments

BlkPoints can only be redeemed in increments of 500 points (£5.00).

5.4 Maximum Redemption Limit

You can use BlkPoints to cover up to 50% of the total value of any booking. The remaining balance must be paid using a standard payment method.

5.5 Minimum Booking Value Requirement

The total value of your booking must be at least twice the value of the BlkPoints you wish to redeem.

For example:
• To redeem £5 in BlkPoints, your booking must total at least £10.
• To redeem £25 in BlkPoints, your booking must total at least £50.

5.6 Automatic Redemption

Eligible BlkPoints will be available for use automatically at checkout. You will be able to choose whether to apply them to your booking during payment.

5.7 Non-Cash Value

BlkPoints have no cash value, cannot be exchanged for money, and may only be redeemed against eligible bookings made through BlkPages.

6. Expiry & Adjustments

6.1 Expiry

BlkPoints expire after 12 months of inactivity (no new points earned or redeemed).

6.2 Adjustments

BlkPages may adjust or remove BlkPoints from your account if:

  • A booking is cancelled or refunded;
  • Points were issued in error; or
  • Fraudulent or abusive activity is suspected.

Account Suspension

BlkPages reserves the right to suspend or terminate your BlkPoints account if you violate these terms or engage in fraudulent activity.
